# Japan SME Subsidies and Employer Grants Admin List

Last checked: 2026-06-17

Use this as an admin checklist for Japanese SME subsidies, employer grants, foreign-worker related support, and official search portals. Deadlines and budgets change often, so always open the official link before preparing an application.

## Fast Search Portals

| Portal | Best for | Link | Admin use |
|---|---|---|---|
| J-Net21 支援情報ヘッドライン | National, prefecture, municipality, and support-organization subsidies, grants, loans, seminars | https://j-net21.smrj.go.jp/snavi/ | Search by region, category, and keyword every month. This is the best official-style source for local programs. |
| ミラサポplus | Main SME Agency / METI subsidy programs and application guidance | https://mirasapo-plus.go.jp/ | Track major national SME grants, guides, examples, and support contacts. |
| Jグランツ | Online submission portal for many national/local grants | https://www.jgrants-portal.go.jp/ | Confirm whether a program requires G Biz ID Prime and electronic filing. |
| GビズID | Required login for many subsidy applications | https://gbiz-id.go.jp/ | Create/maintain G Biz ID Prime before deadlines. |
| e-Gov / e-Stat not usually subsidy portals | Regulations and statistics | https://www.e-gov.go.jp/ | Use only for legal/regulatory confirmation, not daily grant hunting. |

## Local Government Subsidy Tracking

There is no stable single list of every prefecture, city, ward, and town subsidy in Japan. For local programs, admins should maintain a monthly search routine:

1. Search J-Net21 by category "補助金・助成金・融資" and target prefecture/city.
2. Search the prefecture website for: `補助金 中小企業`, `助成金 中小企業`, `設備投資 補助金`, `賃上げ 補助金`, `省エネ 補助金`, `外国人材 助成`.
3. Search the municipality/ward website with the same keywords.
4. Check the local Chamber of Commerce / Society of Commerce.
5. Confirm whether the application is through Jグランツ, email, postal mail, or local portal.

## Admin Checklist Before Applying

| Check | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Confirm eligibility definition of SME / small business | Definitions differ by industry, capital, employees, and affiliated companies. |
| Confirm current call and deadline | Many grants close early or change documents by round. |
| Prepare G Biz ID Prime | Account issuance can take time and is required for many electronic applications. |
| Prepare latest financials and tax certificates | Common requirement for national and local subsidies. |
| Check whether pre-approval is required before ordering | Many subsidies reject costs incurred before grant decision. |
| Check wage increase and minimum wage requirements | Many 2026 programs include wage/productivity conditions. |
| Check conflicts with other subsidies | Same expense often cannot be double-subsidized. |
| Keep quotes, specs, contracts, invoices, proof of payment | Required for inspection and reimbursement. |
| Calendar post-award reporting | Many programs require business performance, wage, asset, or effect reports for several years. |
